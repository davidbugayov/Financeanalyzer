Money Under Control is a modern personal finance management application that helps users track income and expenses, visualize spending structure, and control their financial life.

Key features:
• Add income and expenses with categories and sources
• Detailed analytics with charts and diagrams
• Category-based budgeting with limit tracking
• Import transactions from bank statements (PDF, CSV, Excel)
• Statistics for day, week, month, quarter, and year
• Customizable categories for income and expenses
• Financial indicators: savings rate, financial cushion
• Support for dark/light theme

The application works completely offline, does not require internet, contains no ads and no in-app purchases. All data is stored only on the user's device.
